Welcome to UnSpoken

UnSpoken is your interactive platform for learning sign language through real-time practice and feedback. Our innovative system combines video demonstrations with webcam recognition to help you master sign language effectively.

Key Features

Video Demonstrations

Clear, high-quality video demonstrations for each sign, allowing you to observe and learn the correct hand movements and gestures.

Real-time Practice

Practice signs using your webcam with instant feedback on your performance, helping you perfect your technique.

Progress Tracking

Track your learning progress with mastery badges and completion statistics for each module.

How to Use UnSpoken

  1. Choose a Learning Module

    Start with the Greetings & Basics module to learn essential signs, then progress through other modules like Basic Signs & Numbers and more.

  2. Watch the Demonstration

    Each sign comes with a clear video demonstration. Watch carefully and pay attention to hand positions, movements, and facial expressions if applicable.

  3. Practice with Webcam

    Click "Start Practice" to activate your webcam. Position yourself clearly in the frame and perform the sign. The system will provide real-time feedback on your performance.

  4. Track Your Progress

    Signs are marked as mastered when you perform them correctly. Complete all signs in a module to unlock achievements and progress to more advanced content.

Pro Tips

  • Ensure good lighting when using the webcam
  • Practice each sign multiple times to build muscle memory
  • Use the description provided with each sign for proper technique
  • Take advantage of the dark mode option for comfortable learning in different lighting conditions

Practice Guidelines

For the best learning experience:

  • Practice in a well-lit environment with a clear background
  • Position your webcam at face/upper body level
  • Ensure your hands are clearly visible in the frame
  • Take regular breaks to avoid fatigue
  • Review previously learned signs regularly
  • Use the practice mode frequently to reinforce learning
